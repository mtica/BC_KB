    Use reports to get a bird's eye view of your site's traffic, including visitors location, page views, and referrals. You can select from a number of preset reports or create a custom report.

    View reports

    1. Select Reports > Visitors.
    2. Click through the tabs at the top of the page to view different report types.

    Tip:You can use the export option to get a copy of a report.

    Build a custom report

    1. Select Reports > Custom Reports.
    2. Select one of the following:
      1. For customer data, click Customer Report
      2. For traffic, browser, and geographic data, click Website Report
      3. For sales and orders, click E-commerce Report
    3. Follow the steps to build your custom report.

      • Creating a Custom E-Commerce Report
      • Creating a Customer Report
