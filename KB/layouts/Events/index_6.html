    What is the bookings module?

    You can add an event invitation to your site that lets visitors sign up for the event. Business Catalyst tracks the number of available places. It can also send booking confirmation emails and follow-up email to attendees after the event.

    List of available modules

    • module_booking
    • module_calendar

    List of available layouts

    The module_booking module is always rendered using the List Layout. Only after you click on the links outputted by either the {tag_name} or the {tag_button,Your Text} tags are you redirected to its detail view (rendered using the Detail Layout).
